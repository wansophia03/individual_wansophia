After spm, my result at that time was not that good. so I didn't get an offer to go to university. after that, my school counselor suggested to apply for a pre -diploma. The Pre-Diploma program is offered to anyone with a result of less than 5 credits which makes the student ineligible to continue their studies to the Diploma level. So the result, the application I made was accepted and I got the program offered at UiTM Perak Branch Campus Seri Iskandar. This pre -diploma program ran for 6 months and after that I got the opportunity to apply to university with a pointer of 3.48. So that's how I got a library management course at UiTM Kedah and currently on my last semester.
